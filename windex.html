About Me

I am a passionate computer programmer and graphic designer with a strong desire for developing mobile applications, websties, as well as creating eye-catching and original design solutions. I have been working with Adobe Photoshop for 3 years now and have quickly caught on with Illustrator and Dreamweaver. After building a portfolio of my own personal personal projects for the past 3 years, I am ready to put these skills to the test in order to make your brand or project successful.
